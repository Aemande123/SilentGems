Silent's Gems - Changelog

1.3.0.0 - Beta
- Torch Bandolier and Chaos Gems can no longer be "repaired".
- Torch Bandolier extraction recipe. Just place it in a crafting grid to get a stack of torches out.
- Return Home resets fall distance (no more fall damage when teleporting).
- Added Enchantment Tokens for bows.
- Unbreaking Enchantment Token now works on bows.
