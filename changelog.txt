Silent's Gems - Changelog

1.3.0.0 - Beta
- Torch Bandolier extraction recipe. Just place it in a crafting grid to get a stack of torches out.
- Return Home resets fall distance (no more fall damage when teleporting).
- New enchantment token textures (should make finding the right one a little easier)
- Added Enchantment Tokens for bows.
- Unbreaking and Mending Enchantment Tokens now work on bows.
- Enriched Chaos Essence blocks, because why not?
- Tool mining levels are now configurable.
- (Bugfix) Torch Bandolier and Chaos Gems can no longer be "repaired".
